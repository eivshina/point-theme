About Me

Hi! I'm Katya, an Applied Math PhD student working on AI for medicine in Zitnik lab. I am also a content creator (90k+ followers) and entrepreneur.

My research focuses on developing artificial intelligence methods for medicine and healthcare. I'm particularly interested in using machine learning to improve disease diagnosis, treatment recommendations, and drug discovery.

As a content creator, I share insights about academia, research, and the PhD application process. My goal is to make these topics more accessible and help others navigate their academic journeys.
